Find us...

We are located along Kiserian-Isinya road, approximately 5.4km from Kiserian town.

The most convinient way to get here is by car.

Otherwise, if commuting by matatu from Nairobi, get 126 and disembark at Kiserian. Catch a bodaboda to the garden.
