A recreational garden with breath taking beauty and idyllic atmosphere, Elga Gardens, at oltepesi provides a placid environment with fresh air away from the busy, bustling city.

Elga Gardens was bought and farmed by Samuel Waithaka in 2010. It is currently run by Peter Kiragu. The garden is set within beautiful gardens surrounded by natural and fresh air. With ideal space for various functions including but not limited to :- weddings, parties, private functions, birthdays, exhibitions, camping site...

For Group Functions like; Chama meetings, group fun days, team building, celebrations, our grounds are open.
